My design Process

“The payoff of a customer-centric approach to software and digital product design is substantial and long-lasting for both companies and their customers”
— Alan Cooper
I use and experiment products, often looking on how to improve them. Always defending all users, by exploring the best, inclusive, user experience for everyone. For that, I rely on the double diamond structure to understand customers, their wants and needs, and the issues they face, and explore creative and innovative ways to solve their problems, and delivering great experiences. This process allows enough flexibility to adapt my approach to the project requirements, budget, and timeline, which ensures the best possible results with maximum efficiency.
